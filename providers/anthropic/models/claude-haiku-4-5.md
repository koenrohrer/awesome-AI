## What it is

Claude Haiku 4.5 is Anthropic's fastest current general-purpose model. Anthropic positions it as near-frontier quality at much lower cost and latency than Sonnet and Opus.

## Official system card in Markdown

This is a markdown digest of Anthropic's official system card and launch material, not a verbatim copy.

### Capability and product summary

- Anthropic presents Haiku 4.5 as a fast, cost-efficient model with strong coding and computer-use performance.
- The launch notes say it approaches or exceeds older frontier-quality behavior in some tasks while remaining the cheapest current Claude tier.

### Safety and deployment summary

- Anthropic's official Haiku 4.5 system card reports broad harmlessness and alignment evaluation coverage.
- The public release notes say Anthropic deployed Haiku 4.5 under ASL-2 rather than ASL-3, distinguishing it from stronger Sonnet/Opus tiers.

### Integration notes

- Use Haiku 4.5 for latency-sensitive chat, customer support, or coding-helper tasks.
- For harder reasoning or long-horizon agentic work, move up to Sonnet 4.6 or Opus.

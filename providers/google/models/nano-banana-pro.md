## What it is

Nano Banana Pro, also documented as Gemini 3 Pro Image, is Google's Gemini 3-series image-generation model for high-context visual generation and editing workflows.

## Integration notes

- Google documents Nano Banana Pro as an image generation/editing model, not a general text-output reasoning model.
- The Gemini 3 guide says image-generation and editing workflows strictly validate thought signatures across model parts.
- Use the Gemini models and pricing pages for current resolution, pricing, and preview-status details.

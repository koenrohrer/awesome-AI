## What it is

GPT-5.4 Pro is the high-compute variant of GPT-5.4. OpenAI positions it for users who want maximum performance on the most complex tasks and can tolerate higher latency and price.

## Official system card in Markdown

OpenAI does not currently publish a separate public system card dedicated only to `gpt-5.4-pro`. The closest official safety document is the GPT-5.4 Thinking system card.

### Relevant official coverage in Markdown

- GPT-5.4 Pro is presented as part of the GPT-5.4 launch family.
- The official model page says it uses more compute to think harder and may take several minutes on some requests.
- For safety interpretation, use the GPT-5.4 family system-card guidance unless OpenAI publishes a Pro-specific addendum.
